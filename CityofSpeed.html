City of Speed

City is fast. Everything is fast. In this project, I want to convey the feeling of fast and tense of Hong Kong and discover some social issues.

Previous Next

Fast Dining 速食

Urban people tend to work overtime, which leads to lack of sleep. This also requires speeding up the rhythm of other aspects of life, including eating fast food, instant noodles and caffeinated beverages, and even relying on various drugs to keep the body running quickly; Fill your stomach without any nutrition.

都市人傾向超時工作，引致睡眠不足，更為此而要求加快生活其他方面的節奏，包括飲食傾向快餐、即食麵和含咖啡因的飲料，甚至依賴各種藥物令身體可以不斷快速運轉；自求填飽肚子，沒有營養。

Fast ​​Learning 速學

School children’s after-school activities, including tuition and various after-school interest classes, are also extremely frequent, causing children to have time-critical issues early; the media requires scholars to provide immediate and concise "analysis", creating various types of "quick Thinker"- a person who can provide instant answers to any question. 

學童的課餘活動，包括補習和各式課後興趣班的安排，也極之頻繁，令兒童提早出現時間緊迫問題；傳媒則要求學者能提供即時而簡要的「分析」，造就了各類「快速思考家」——對任何問題都能夠提供即時答案的人。

Fast Walking 速走

Rest and leisure have also been transformed into urgency activities, or "quick results" slimming keep fit exercise packages; working overtime and lack of sleep; fast transportation; quick action.

休憩餘閒也轉化為急就章式的活動，如走馬看花式的旅行，或「快速見效」的纖體keep fit運動套餐；超時工作，睡眠不足；快速的交通工具；快速的行動。
